Bringing cinema lovers together

Watch and discuss new films together over food & drink

Watch. Discuss. Eat.

We know it’s hard to meet like-minded people these days. Why not meet your fellow cinephiles to watch the latest film and discuss them over food and drink?

What’s better than building community over the latest blockbuster?

How it works:

Each month we send out an email with a list of local showings as well as food options. We’ll confirm the film and location with the most amount votes by the second week.

Any concerns, let us know through the Ornellion Cinema Club Discord.

We make sure to ensure there are alcohol-free options and have at least 3 alcohol-free evenings each month.
